# Analysis Summary

- **Total Listings**: ~49,000 properties
- **Location**: New York City (2019)
- **Data Quality**: 38,843 records after cleaning

## Key Statistical Insights

### Pricing Analysis
- **Average Price**: $152.72 per night
- **Price Range**: $0 - $10,000
- **Most Expensive Borough**: Manhattan ($196.88 avg)

### Geographic Insights

- **Manhattan**: Highest prices but fewer listings
- **Popular Neighborhoods**: Williamsburg, Bedford-Stuyvesant, Harlem

### Availability & Reviews

- **Peak Activity**: Summer months show higher booking rates
